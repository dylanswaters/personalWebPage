Biography

  Dylan Waters has been working with computers since his birth, when he learned to use Windows 96. Graduating from playing with MS-Paint, Dylan began trying to learn more about computers, and started down the road of living in a basement forever. Strongly believing in personal freedom, Dylan found himself attracted to Open-source philosophy and projects. Today, Dylan seeks to learn as much as he can about development and workings of computers.

  Dylan enjoys playing videogames, and with his dogs. He is constantly planning new adventures for his Dungeons and Dragons groups. He enjoys death metal and all other kinds of music, but is accused of being a Satanist anyway. He is a minimalist, and a devout follower of his heart who feels weird writing about himself in third person. His dream is to have a beard long enough to carry a cat around in it.

  Dylan aspires to work for an open-source organization or a non-profit, such as Wikipedia. Dylan's choice of Operating System is Arch Linux.
